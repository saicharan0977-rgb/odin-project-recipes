Paparika omlette



Eggs are often considered the second best item on the planet , its unique flavour and its texture is often quoted to be matchless and its supreme in every right


Its very simple to make a recipe such as a Paparika omlette


Here are the ingrediants needed for making this :

  • Eggs
  • spices
  • Oil
  • Paparika
  • Onion

So after gathereing all the ingrediants get ready on our next ride

Here are the steps on making paparika omlette :

  1. Boil the oil
  2. Add 4 eggs onto the pan on low heat
  3. Let them sit for 2 minutes
  4. Add fresh cleaned paprika pieces to the mixture
  5. After sauting for a while add spices
  6. Add a bit of salt to add flavour
  7. Leave the bowl for 3-5min until water comes together

Serving tips

  • Always put the bowl cover on to let steam help cook teh recipe
  • Dont forget that eggs must from a trusted and safe store
  • Food must be served hot to make the full use of aroma
