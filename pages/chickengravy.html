Chicken Gravy



Chicken is often considered the best item on the planet , its unique flavour and its texture is often quoted to be matchless and its supreme in every right


Its very simple to make a recipe such as a chicken gravy


Here are the ingrediants needed for making this :

  • Chicken
  • spices
  • Oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion

So after gathereing all the ingrediants get ready on our next ride

Here are the steps on making chicken gravy :

  1. Boil the oil
  2. Add freshly chopped onion and tomatoes pieces into the water
  3. Let them sit for 2 minutes
  4. Add fresh cleaned chicken pieces to the mixture
  5. After sauting for a while add spices
  6. Add a cup of water to add volume
  7. Leave the bowl fopr 3-5min until water comes together

Serving tips

  • Always put the bowl cover on to let steam help cook teh recipe
  • Dont forget that chicken must from a trusted and safe store
  • Food must be served hot to make the full use of aroma
