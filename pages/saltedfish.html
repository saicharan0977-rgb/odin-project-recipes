Fried fish is a very uniquely tasting food , its unique flavour and its texture is often quoted to be good and its better in every right


Its very simple to make a recipe such as a Fried fish


Here are the ingrediants needed for making this :

  • Fish
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Mint leaves
  • Onion

So after gathereing all the ingrediants get ready on our next ride

Here are the steps on making fired fish :

  1. Boil the oil
  2. Add freshly chopped onion and fish pieces into the oil
  3. Let them sit for 2 minutes
  4. Add salt to the mixture
  5. After sauting for a while add spices
  6. Add 2-3 leaves of mint to add flavour
  7. Leave the bowl for 3-5min until colour comes together

Serving tips

  • Always put the bowl cover on to let steam help cook teh recipe
  • Don't forget that fish must from a trusted and safe store
  • Food must be served hot to make the full use of aroma
